Inception is a science fiction thriller about a skilled thief who enters people's dreams to steal secrets from their subconscious. He is given a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully plant an idea into someone's mind, a process called inception.
The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, along with Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, and Ken Watanabe.
